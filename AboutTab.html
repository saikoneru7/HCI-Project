Saint Louis Art Museum

The Saint Louis Art Museum is one of the principal U.S. art museums, with paintings, sculptures, cultural objects, and ancient masterpieces from all corners of the world. Its threestory building stands in Forest Park in St. Louis, Missouri, where it is visited by up to a half million people every year.
